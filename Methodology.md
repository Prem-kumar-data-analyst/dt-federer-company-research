# Research Methodology

## Step 1 — Define ICP

The target profile was:
- promoter-led
- specialty manufacturer
- technically differentiated
- growth-oriented
- India-based

---

## Step 2 — Build Initial Universe

Initial sourcing was done through:
- LinkedIn
- Tracxn
- Google search
- biotech directories
- pharma manufacturing lists
- DSIR-recognized companies
- startup databases

Approximately 80+ companies were reviewed.

---

## Step 3 — Qualification

Each company was checked against:
- manufacturing capability
- technical differentiation
- founder background
- sector tailwinds
- growth signals

---

## Step 4 — Federer Scoring

Companies were scored using:
- C1 to C6 framework
- Weak / Moderate / Strong evidence
- weighted scoring model

---

## Step 5 — Validation

All major claims were manually checked using:
- official websites
- LinkedIn
- public articles
- regulatory mentions
- company news

Unsupported claims were removed.
